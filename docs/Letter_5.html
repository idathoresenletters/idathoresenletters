September 9, 1931.

Miss Ida von Schulzenheim,
Riddaregatan 78,
Stockholm, Sweden.

Dear Miss von Schulzenheim:

Dividing row of colons and hyphens


As yet I have been unable to do anything in the Bendix matter.
His private affairs have not been settled and until these have been
cleared up it seems impossible to do anything with him. “Soldyrkaren”
and “Crocus” have both been returned to the Building.

Dividing row of colons and hyphens


Regretting the delay in replying to your letters, and with
best wishes and greetings, I am

Sincerely yours,


Amandus Johnson.


2022 Charlotte Selander and Sofia Rödström.
