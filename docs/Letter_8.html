UTDRAG

JOHN MORTON MEMORIAL MUSEUM
Nineteenth St. and Pattison Ave.
Philadelphia, Penn.

October 25, 1934.


To His Excellency,
Minister W. Bostrom,
The Swedish Legation,
Washington, D. C.

Dear Minister Bostrom:

Three dots


I expect to go to Washington before long to do some work in
the Library of Congress in connection with my two forthcoming books
and hope I may be able to see you then. I should like to show you
two or three letters from Dr. Hoving in Sweden and ask you what can
be done about it.

Three dots


Sincerely yours,

AMANDUS JOHNSON.
