ROYAL V. CONSULATE OF SWEDEN
AT PHILADELPHIA

PHILADELPHIA.
, May 9, 1936


IN REPLY PLEASE REFER TO NO.

Miss Ida C. Thoresen .
Karlavägen 56,
Stockholm, Sweden.

Dear Madam:

I duly received your letter of April
3rdinquiring about your statue "Soldyrkaren."

I have written two or three letters to
Dr. Johnson and have just received a reply from
him advising me that the statue is stored in one
of the wings of the Museum; that he has talked to
a number of people about it and has hopes of ex-
hibiting it in Brooklyn, where it might be sold
“if Miss Thoresen is willing to reduce her price."
Dr. Johnson advises me he has received some letters
from you but unfortunately has not been in a pos-
ition to answer them. I understand he will write
to you very soon. I would suggest that you write
him regarding his suggestion as to a reduction in
price.

If there is anything further I may do for
you, please do not hesitate to write me.

Yours very truly,

MAH: EMC

Vice Consul

2022 Charlotte Selander and Sofia Rödström.
